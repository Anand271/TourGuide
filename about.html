Introduction:


Once me and some of my friends were talking, then a friend of mine said that there is no places to visit in Raigarh city, at that time I did not say anything to him but I felt bad that how could anyone talk like this about my city And then when I came to know that I have to make a website for my major project then I thought why not make a website for different tourist places of Raigarh and started working on this website.



Objective:


This project will help to know the best tourst places in the Raigarh city. In which I have developed an “online Raigarh tourist management system” website through which you can find different tourist places to explore the Raigarh city, and some amazing pictures , speciality, location link of that places wich is helpful to decide your favourite place to visit.The main purpose of this project is to give the information about such place where you want to visit
